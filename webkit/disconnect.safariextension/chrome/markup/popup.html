Browse the web normally. As you do, the graph in this popup and the counter in the toolbar will update. Each circle in the graph represents a site that has or would’ve been sent some of your personal info.

Circles with a halo are sites you’ve visited. Circles without a halo are sites you haven’t.

Circles in red are known tracking sites. Circles in gray aren’t, but may still track you.

Mouse over a circle to view that site’s tracking footprint. Click a red circle to block or unblock that site.
